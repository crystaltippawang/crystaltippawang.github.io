ABOUT US

Unite US is a website that aims to target and educate fifth graders in United States geography. All throughout elementary school, different parts of social studies have been presented as a foundation for students heading into middle school. Students will use an interactive map to learn about the differences between the states, and how all together they make one strong nation: the United States. Along with the map, we also have additional fun activities that will help support what they learned.

TO NAVIGATE THE SITE, please click the arrows by the top left of the page to go up or down.
